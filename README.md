# Pong
The 20 Games Challenge, Game 1: Pong
# What your project is/does (and what it’s called)
Its a pong game heres some of the things it has:
an arena with two walls and a divider.
a paddle on either end of the play field. Use player inputs to move the paddles up and down.
a ball that moves around the playfield and bounces off of the paddles and walls.
Detect when the ball leaves the playfield. Assign a point to the player who scored.
Track and display the score for each player.
an AI script that can follow the ball so you can play with only one player.
A menu and allow the player to reset the game.
Some basic sounds. Play a sound every time the ball collides with something, and every time a player scores.
